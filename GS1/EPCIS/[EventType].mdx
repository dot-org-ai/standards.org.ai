## Event Structure

EPCIS events follow a standard structure with core fields:
- **What**: Which objects (EPCs) are involved
- **When**: The date and time of the event
- **Where**: The location where the event occurred
- **Why**: Business step and disposition indicating the business context

## Usage

{eventType.name} is used in EPCIS to track supply chain events. Each event type has specific fields appropriate to its purpose.
