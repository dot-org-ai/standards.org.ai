### Basic Usage in Turtle

```turtle
@prefix rdf: <http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#> .
@prefix ex: <http://example.org/> .

ex:subject {Property.uri} ex:object .

# With literal value

ex:multiValue {Property.uri} ex:value1 ,

                            ex:value3 .
```

### Usage in RDF/XML

```xml
<rdf:RDF xmlns:rdf="http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#"

  <rdf:Description rdf:about="http://example.org/subject">
    <{Property.uri} rdf:resource="http://example.org/object"/>
```
